Collision rules

What happens when two ranks overlap? Edit the upper triangle — the rest mirrors.

  • Pass through — ignore each other
  • Bounce — reverse directions
  • Annihilate — both disappear
  • Absorb — higher rank eats lower
  • Redirect — swap move axes
  • Merge — combine into a higher rank
